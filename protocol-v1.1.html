Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2026 · Version 1.0
Your privacy is the foundation of Protocol. Your health data never leaves your device during normal use.
What stays on your device (always)
Dose logs, body composition, check-ins, protocol structure, pantry data, settings, and journal entries. None of this is ever transmitted during normal use.
When data leaves your device
AI Reviews (Pro only): When you tap “Run AI Review,” your protocol structure — compound names, doses, schedule, and compliance — is sent to Anthropic's Claude API. Your name, email, and biometric data are never included. Anthropic does not train on API data by default.
License validation (Pro only): Your license key is sent to our server to confirm Pro status. No health data included.
Withings sync (optional): Body composition data is pulled from Withings servers. Your credentials are stored in Cloudflare's encrypted storage, not in Protocol. Retrieved data is stored locally.
Analytics
We use Plausible Analytics — no cookies, no personal data, no individual tracking.
Deleting your data
Settings → Reset to Factory Settings deletes everything. For data sent to third parties (Anthropic, Withings), deletion is governed by their privacy policies.
Protocol is not intended for anyone under 18. Questions? Contact us through the Protocol website.
