Privacy Policy
Last updated: April 2026 · Version 1.0
Your privacy is the foundation of Protocol. Your health data never leaves your device during normal use. This policy tells you exactly when and why that changes — so you can make informed decisions.
What stays on your device (always)
All of the following is stored exclusively on your device using your browser's local storage. It is never transmitted to our servers or any third party during normal use: your compound logs and dose history; body composition entries; check-in notes; protocol structure and schedules; pantry stock levels; settings and preferences; all journal entries.
When data leaves your device
Data leaves your device only in these specific circumstances, all of which require your explicit action:
AI Protocol Reviews (Pro only): When you tap "Run AI Review," your protocol structure — compound names, doses, schedule, and compliance percentage — is sent to Anthropic's Claude API. Your name, email, biometric measurements, and financial data are never included. Anthropic processes this data to generate your review and does not use API data for model training by default. See Anthropic's privacy policy at anthropic.com/privacy.
License Validation (Pro only): Your license key is sent to our validation server to confirm your Pro status. No health data is included in this request.
Withings Sync (optional): If you connect Withings, your body composition data is pulled from Withings servers via an OAuth integration. Your Withings credentials are stored securely in Cloudflare's encrypted key-value storage, not in Protocol. The data retrieved from Withings is stored locally on your device.
Analytics
Protocol uses privacy-respecting analytics (Plausible Analytics) that do not use cookies and do not collect personal data. We track aggregate usage events such as feature adoption rates. No individual user is identified or tracked.
Data deletion
Since your data lives on your device, you can delete it at any time by using Settings → Reset to Factory Settings, or by clearing your browser/app data. For any data transmitted to third parties (Anthropic API, Withings), deletion is governed by their respective privacy policies.
Children's privacy
Protocol is not intended for use by anyone under the age of 18. We do not knowingly collect any information from children.
Questions about your privacy? Contact us through the Protocol website. This policy may be updated periodically. We will notify users of material changes.
